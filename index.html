How to engage your twitter Followers

Assembly time: 10mins
Awesome rating: 1,000,000% awesome

Are you wondering how to get more followers and to engage your twitter audience? Then follow these steps and you will be gaining traction in no time!

  • Things You'll Need

  • A Twitter Account
  • A device to access the internet
  • A beautiful, sharp mind
  • Content to write about that is important to you
  • A photo that relates to your content
  • Knowledge on relevant hashtags

  1. Choose something that is interesting to write about.

    To engage your followers write about a topic that other people will want to read.

  2. Write a thick tweet about your topic.

    Reader's need some substance and content to engage with. Write a tweet that is 3-5 sentences long about your chosen topic.

  3. Add a relevant photo to your post.

    The human mind is a very frantic and delicate creature. To optomize the engagement of your post you need to give these needy human eyes something to look at.

  4. Once you have added a photo, your last step is to add timely hashtags.

    Twitter uses can find your post by clicking and searching these hashtags. Write a hashtag that is trending or meaningful to you or your community.

  5. Become the most popular tweeter in America.

    Now that you have followed all of the steps watch your follower count shoot to the moon!

    Next thing you know you will be able to have more power to polarize your community than the very best twitter influencers!

    Fix the pump tube to it with plasticine.
